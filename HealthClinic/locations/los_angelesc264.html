Fines and Costs

A traffic ticket lawyer Los Angeles can help you better understand the fines and costs that are assessed with a traffic ticket. In California, a 20 percent surcharge is added, and additional fees may include:

  • County penalty assessments
  • State penalty assessments
  • Emergency medical services penalty assessment
  • Court facility construction penalty assessment
  • DNA identification fund penalty assessment

The total fine for your ticket will vary throughout the state and county based on your age, your driving record, the severity of the offense and the type of offense. You have the option to pay the ticket and plead guilty or no contest, which includes paying for traffic school, paying the fine, providing proof of correction for a fix-it ticket, adding points to your driving record and possibly dealing with an increase to your insurance premiums.

With a ticket lawyer Los Angeles, you can fight your ticket and plead not guilty. This means you have a choice to hire an attorney or represent yourself in court. You may also attend a court trial conducted by a judicial officer, have a trial by mail or a “written declaration” and you may also have to pay legal and court fees. At The Ticket Clinic California, we encourage you to enter the battle with a professional on your side to avoid any of these inconveniences.
