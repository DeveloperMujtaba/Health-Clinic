California Ticket Penalties

If you are convicted for a moving traffic violation, the state of California will add points to your driving record, which can affect many aspects of your ability to drive. There are times when the points can be waived if you agree to go to traffic school. With a San Diego traffic attorney working with you, you better understand what your best option is when it is presented by the court.

Convictions for things like making an unsafe lane change, speeding or at-fault accidents result in one point on your record. Reckless driving, DUI, hit and run accidents or driving with a revoked or suspended license may result in two points on your record. The more points you acquire in a short period, the more likely you are to lose your license. With the help of a San Diego traffic ticket lawyer, you can avoid the whole headache altogether.
